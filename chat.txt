❯ how to activate on windows                                                    

● On Windows the venv puts its scripts in Scripts/, not bin/. Since you're in
  Git Bash:

  source venv/Scripts/activate

  For other shells:

  - PowerShell: venv\Scripts\Activate.ps1 (if blocked by execution policy, run
  Set-ExecutionPolicy -Scope CurrentUser RemoteSigned once)
  - cmd.exe: venv\Scripts\activate.bat

  Once active, your prompt will show (venv) at the start.
