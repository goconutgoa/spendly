Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly (the "Service") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly to us when you create an account and use the Service. This includes your account details, such as your name and email address, and the expense data you enter, such as amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect basic technical information automatically, such as your browser type and the pages you visit, to help us keep the Service running smoothly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Service. This means displaying your expenses, generating spending summaries and category breakdowns, and maintaining your account. We do not sell your personal or financial information to third parties, and we do not use your expense data for advertising. We may use aggregated, anonymized information to understand how the Service is used and to make it better.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is associated with your account. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. While no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, we work to safeguard your data and retain it only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on trusted third-party providers for infrastructure such as hosting and data storage. These providers only process your information on our behalf and are obligated to protect it. We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes. If the Service integrates additional third-party tools in the future, we will update this policy to reflect how they are used.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the Service. We will do our best to address your questions and respond to any requests regarding your personal information.
